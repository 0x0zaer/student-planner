Instructions for Uploading Your Notes and Study Materials

Students can upload a variety of study materials, including lecture notes, assignment submissions, and reference files, to ensure easy access and sharing of educational resources. Whether it's detailed class notes, completed assignments, research papers, or supplementary reading materials, uploading these files allows for better organization and collaboration among peers. Accepted formats may include PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, and text files, making it convenient to share and review content anytime. By contributing study materials, students can support each other’s learning while building a comprehensive repository of academic resources.

Steps for file upload
  1. Go to select file type option.
  2. Click on Select File or Browse to open your device’s file manager.
  3. Choose the document you want to upload (supported formats: .pdf, .docx, images, .txt, etc.).
  4. Click Open to attach the file.
  5. After selection press the Submit button.
